Domain 3: Instruction

This domain focuses on how teachers actively engage students in learning, use questioning and discussion effectively, assess understanding, and respond to student needs in real time. Principals look for lessons that are purposeful, interactive, and responsive.

3a: Communicating with Students

What principals look for: Clear directions, learning goals shared with students, and explanations suited to the learners’ age and level.

Your Example: Describe how you use “I can” statements, visuals, or modeling to clarify learning expectations for your students.
