Overview

Domain 1 focuses on a teacher’s ability to plan effective instruction based on standards, student needs, and available resources. Principals look for evidence that lessons are intentional, data-driven, and designed for all learners.

1a: Demonstrating Knowledge of Content and Pedagogy

Prompt: How do you demonstrate understanding of the content you teach and effective teaching strategies?

Example: Explain how you adapt instruction to different learning styles or integrate cross-curricular connections.
