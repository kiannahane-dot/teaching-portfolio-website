Hello! My name is Kianna Cornell, and welcome to my teaching portfolio. This site highlights my philosophy, classroom management, and professional growth across the four Danielson Domains. It also serves as a space to showcase lesson plans, reflections, and instructional design.

Teaching Philosophy

Education is a journey of growth, curiosity, and connection. I believe every child has the ability to succeed when they are supported, encouraged, and challenged in meaningful ways. My goal as an educator is to foster a learning environment that empowers students to take risks, celebrate progress, and love learning.
